Welcome to INTELLCAP Innovation Days Subscription & Evaluation Form

Innovation Days is an intensive five-day forum dedicated to learning by doing/hands-on though showcasing cutting-edge projects in the fields of health and sports, with a focus on Artificial Intelligence (AI), IoT/sensors, Digital Twins and Metaverse. This evaluation form is designed to carefully assess and select the most promising projects aligned with these themes. Our goal is to determine if these projects are comprehensive, innovative, and performant enough to be presented to our esteemed experts collaborators, sponsors, and stakeholders, mainly seed funding providers and investors.

We kindly ask you to be truthful and sincere in your evaluations and presentations, allowing us to assess each project fairly and impartially. By doing so, we can ensure that only the best and most impactful innovations are brought to the forefront during the event. Not covering all the criteria shall not be seen as a handicap, instead, it shall be considered as an opportunity for empowerment of your skills. You are invited to express freely your specific needs and the outcomes you are seeking for.
